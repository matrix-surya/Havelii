Our Story

From the Highways to Your Heart

The idea behind Haveli was simple: to combine the warmth of a traditional Indian dhaba with the elegance of royal cuisine. Just like a dhaba welcomes travelers with open arms, Haveli was created to welcome food lovers from all walks of life—those seeking authentic Indian flavors wrapped in love and hospitality.

Here, every flavor tells a story, and every meal is a celebration. Whether it’s the zing of freshly ground spices, the richness of buttery gravies, or the crunch of perfectly baked breads, every bite connects you to the stories of India’s vibrant culinary heritage.

Special Dish

Chicken Afghan Curry

Afghanistan is renowned for its love of slow-cooked, aromatic food that brings out the natural flavors of every ingredient. Chicken Afghan Curry takes inspiration from the hearty meals served in Afghan households and royal courts. The dish combines the simplicity of traditional cooking methods with the richness of ingredients like yogurt, cream, and a medley of spices. The influence of the Silk Road trade routes can be felt in this dish, as Afghan cuisine beautifully marries the flavors of Central Asia, Persia, and the Indian subcontinent. This dish represents a journey—a story of cultural exchange and culinary innovation.
